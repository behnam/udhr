Orthography used in this translation

The only difference between the orthographies presented in [UCC and UMC] and in [Hartell] seems to be the use of U+1EA1 ạ LATIN SMALL LETTER A WITH DOT BELOW in the former, and U+028C ʌ LATIN SMALL LETTER TURNED V in the latter. In this translation, we have used the latter, as it seems more modern. It is also used, e.g., at http://www.visitsierraleone.org/temne.asp.
